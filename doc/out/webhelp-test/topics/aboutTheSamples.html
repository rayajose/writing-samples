Cryptographic key management

My previous employer was an e-commerce services firm. One of our clients was a major financial institution. We hosted the client's points redemption shopping site. The site was a marketplace with various retail store partners. Card members used points to buy products from the retail partners.

Two of my primary documentation responsibilities was Information Security Policy and Procedure and Change Management Processes.

The Information Security Policies were used as part of annual, third-party audits for Payment Card Industry Data Security Standard compliance (PCI DSS). The sample provided is a Cryptographic Key Management Policy written to the PCI DSS framework.

The process document describes retail partner B2B system integration to the shopping site.

The samples provided are not from actual internal documents. They are documents based on experience and recent research for the sole purpose of creating the samples.
