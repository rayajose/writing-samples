Management Team

Members

The team is made up of management and functional area leads.

  • Management

    • IT Director

    • Marketing Director

    • Program Manager

    • Engagement Manager

    • Release Manager

  • Functional Areas

    • Development Lead

    • Test Lead

    • Operations Lead

Roles

Any team member can make a change request and provide input in the approval process. However, official approval comes from the Client and/or the IT Director. Changes are implemented by functional area team members.

Important: The Approver and Implementer of a change request cannot be the same person.

  • Approvers: Review a change request to determine if it is ready for implementation. They consider the change's scope, benefits, and potential risks. The approver may authorize, reject, or return the request for more information.

  • Implementers: Execute the approved change, aiming to minimize disruption. There may be multiple implementers for a single change request, each with their own assigned tasks.

Roadmap Meetings

The Company roadmap meeting is a strategic planning session where the management team will define and discuss organizational goals. The goal of the meeting is to establish a shared understanding across teams.

Discussions will typically cover the following areas:

  • prospective work

  • work in progress

  • changes to existing processes, systems, applications

Meeting agenda typically includes the following actions:

  • defining objectives

  • setting milestones

  • ensuring alignment

  • discussing strategy

  • getting buy-in and greenlight
