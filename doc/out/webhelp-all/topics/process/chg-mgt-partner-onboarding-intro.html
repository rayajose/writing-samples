Shop with Points Partner Onboarding

Process

Overview

This process describes partner onboarding to the Client's Shop with Points website. It involves establishing the B2B integration between partner and Company backend systems. The points of integration with our system include the following:

  • daily import of product catalog data from the partner system

  • automated order placement into the partner system

Scope

Process scope is limited to system integration points covered in the overview. Onboarding will include one partner per project. If multiple onboardings are scheduled simultaneously, they are managed as separate projects. The Engagement Manager will drive each project to completion. Completion of an onboarding results in the partner's product catalog available on the shopping site for purchase.

Stakeholders

Members of these groups share a stake in this process and will also own the tasks involved.

Client business contact
Partner business and technical contacts and/or market affiliate contact
Internal Teams Engagement Manager and area leads

Timeline

Projects are generally planned to take 4 weeks to complete. Launch dates are generally firm. When multiple onboardings occur simultaneously, project schedules should be staggered accordingly.

Confirm Partner Requirements

Business Requirements

Gather information about the prospective partner. The partner must meet business requirements to join the program and have policies in place for supporting sale of their products on the site.

  1. Verify the partner meets minimum program requirements to join the program.

  2. Gather partner contact information along with their store policies. This information will be used later in the process.

System Requirements

The partner's system must support B2B backend integration with our system.

  • Analyze the partner's product data to confirm required specifications.

  • Analyze the partner's order placement system to confirm integration feasibility with our system.

Initialize the System

After business and system requirements have been confirmed, the partner must be initialized into our system. System initialization involves preparing the database and configuring management tools to store and process the partner's general information and product catalog data.

Important: Unless instructed otherwise, the code developed for this work should be completed and deployed to all environments in the appropriate sequence: Test, Staging, Production.

Import Product Data

Product data review cycle is complete and file delivery method is in place.

  • Import partner product data.

Implement the Order Placement Controller

The development team has determined the partner order placement service can be integrated.

  • Implement the order placement controller. Enable Add-to-Cart functionality and order placement up to the Final Submit step.

Run a System Build

The partner product data has been successfully loaded into the system.

  • Manually run a system build.

  • Confirm that the internal product management tools and shopping site display the data and function as expected.

Validation

Validate Internal Tools and Shopping Site

The partner product data has been successfully loaded into the system.

  • Confirm that the internal product management tools and shopping site display the data and function as expected.

Setup Store Branding and Landing Page

Once the partner information and product data displays and function as expected on the site, set up the store branding and landing pages.

Place a Test Order

Confirm a successful system integration by placing a test order.
