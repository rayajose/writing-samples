AES-256

Advanced Encryption Standard. The AES encryption algorithm, also known as the Rijndael algorithm, is a type of symmetric block cipher that works with 128-bit blocks of data. AES-256 encryption uses the 256-bit key length to encrypt as well as decrypt a block of messages.
