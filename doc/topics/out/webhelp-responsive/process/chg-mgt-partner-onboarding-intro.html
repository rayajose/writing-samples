    Partner Onboarding

    Overview

    This process involves establishing the B2B integration between and backend systems. The points of integration with our system include the following:

    • daily import of product catalog data from the system

    • automated order placement into the system

    Scope

    Process scope is limited to system integration points covered in the overview. Onboarding will include one per project. If multiple onboardings are scheduled simultaneously, they are managed as separate projects. The will drive each project to completion. Completion of an onboarding results in the product catalog available on the shopping site for purchase.

    Stakeholders

    Members of these groups share a stake in this process and will also own the tasks involved.

    Client business contact
    Partner business and technical contacts and/or market affiliate contact
    Internal Teams and area leads

    Timeline

    Projects are generally planned to take 4 weeks to complete. Launch dates are generally firm. When multiple onboardings occur simultaneously, project schedules should be staggered accordingly.
